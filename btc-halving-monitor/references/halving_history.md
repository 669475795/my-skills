## Key Patterns

### Diminishing Returns
Each cycle's peak gain is roughly 1/3 to 1/4 of the previous. Extrapolating:
- 4th cycle peak gain estimate: 200-400% → $190k-$320k range

### Timeline Pattern
- Peak typically occurs 370-550 days after halving
- Bear market bottom typically 12-14 months after peak
- Prime accumulation zone: 6-18 months before halving

### Hash Ribbon Signals

The Hash Ribbon uses 30-day and 60-day moving averages of hash rate:

| Signal | Condition | Meaning | Historical Accuracy |
|--------|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| Buy | 30d MA crosses above 60d MA | Miner capitulation ended, recovery starting | ~90% hit rate for long-term entries |
| Sell | 30d MA crosses below 60d MA | Miners shutting down, stress beginning | Early warning, not always immediate drop |

### Miner Economics Post-Halving

After each halving, miners' revenue per block is cut 50%. This creates a survival pressure:
1. **Weeks 1-8**: Inefficient miners with high electricity costs begin shutting down
2. **Weeks 8-16**: Hash rate drops as old-gen ASICs become unprofitable
3. **Difficulty adjusts down**: Remaining miners become more profitable
4. **Hash rate recovers**: New equilibrium forms

If price rises enough to offset the reward cut, the capitulation phase is shorter.
If price stagnates or drops, capitulation is deeper and longer — historically a strong buy zone.

| Early Accumulation | 0-100 | Sideways, miner adjustment | DCA spot |

| Acceleration | 300-500 | Parabolic moves possible | Full position, trail stops |
